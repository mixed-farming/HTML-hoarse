My 1x5x10 dimensional companion

Only to you I have described my happiness,
Only to you I have described my glumness.

No one else could see the decelerating drop of tears laying down a wet rail over my cheeks but you.
There were none around me to hear my humdrum yet heart-breaking melancholies but you.

It was only when I used to be with you those nostalgic memories used to pop up like beams.
It was you who always helped me to get back the lost parts of my sweet dreams.

I have always slept beside you without fail;
Those moments were just inexplicable hours of sail!

Your hug at those sorrowful moments was soothing;
And I reciprocating your hug at those ecstatic times must have been choking!

Gift of God, you are so fluffy;
Sorry for those days when I pissed over you like a puppy!

You hear my silence,
You feel my presence,
I appreciate your non-responsive trait;
I've prayed so that you too can speak...until then I wait!

Glad that my secret holding buddy is a wonderful being;
Sad that Creator made my 1×5×10 dimensional companion a non living.

~These lines are dedicated to my side pillow~

-Manoj M Mallya
